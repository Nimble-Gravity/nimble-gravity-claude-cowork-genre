01 — The case for change

The work is changing — on our terms

Agentic AI can now do real, multi-step document work, not just answer questions. The choice in front of every reinsurer is whether that capability is adopted deliberately, with the right guardrails — or ad hoc, without them. Gen Re is choosing deliberate.

01
The capability is real now
Cowork drafts memos, summarizes cedent submissions, and triages queues across your own files — work that used to take hours.
02
Deliberate beats ad hoc
Done right, with scoped access and human sign-off, it's safer and more consistent than people quietly using unapproved tools.
03
It's how we stay sharp
As a reinsurer, our edge is doing knowledge work faster and better. This is one of the clearest ways to protect that edge.
02 — What's in it for you

The payoff is different by role

This isn't one benefit for everyone — it's a specific one for your day.

Analysts & associates

Hours back on memos, summaries, and diligence — so your time goes to the analysis and judgment that only you can do.

Operations & service

Faster turnarounds and more consistent output on the repetitive, document-heavy work that fills the day.

Risk & compliance

Consistency and a clear human-in-the-loop trail — plus a seat at the table in how this is governed, from day one.

Managers

Capacity for your team without adding headcount, and a concrete way to develop people on a skill Gen Re values. See the manager kit →

03 — Let's be direct

This augments you — it doesn't replace you

The honest concern people have about AI is whether it's coming for their job. Here's our straight answer.

You stay in charge

Cowork drafts; you decide

Every output is a draft a person reviews and signs off. The judgment, the relationship, and the accountability stay with you. Nothing goes out the door on its own.

Human-in-the-loop isn't a slogan here; it's the design.
It makes you more valuable

More of your time on the hard part

The people who thrive will be the ones who learn to direct this well — which is exactly what these four workshops teach. This is a skill Gen Re wants you to build.

We're not measuring you by this. We're investing in you with it.
