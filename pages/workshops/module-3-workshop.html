Stage 1 · Pre-work

Bring a workflow to automate

Ten minutes before the session so you arrive with the raw material for your skill.

📋
Before the session

Bring one repetitive workflow worth automating — ideally the one you named after Workshop 2. Know what good output looks like and what should never happen. You'll play the lab in teams of 2–4, so no other prep is needed. Full before & after list on the pre-work & homework page.

Stage 2 · Workshop content · what you'll leave with

Three outcomes for the session

You finish having shipped your first skill from a workflow you actually run.

Decompose a workflow

You can break a repetitive task into steps, data sources, and a clear picture of good versus bad output — the blueprint a skill is built from.

Know a good skill from a bad one

You can hold a SKILL.md against Anthropic's standards: keyword-rich description, under 500 lines, good/bad examples, evals before docs.

Escaped the Skill Vault

Your team has invoked, repaired, authored, and chained skills under a 40-minute clock — and homework ships a .skill for one of your own banking workflows.
