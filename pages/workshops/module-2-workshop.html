Stage 2 · Workshop content · what you'll leave with

Three outcomes for the session

You finish having run a real task and named the workflow you'll turn into a skill next week.

Know your first use cases

A shortlist of reinsurance use cases worth starting with — an underwriting summary from a cedent submission, a cedent or portfolio relationship summary, a claims queue triage — each a draft an underwriter or claims professional signs off on.

Work effectively & safely

Default to Sonnet, steer with permission modes, stay alert to prompt injection, and grant folder access with least privilege.

A real deliverable

You've run one real, document-heavy task to a finished output and noted the recurring part worth turning into a skill in Workshop 3.
