Stage 2 · Workshop content · what you'll leave with

Three outcomes for the session

You finish with a sponsor-ready, one-page rollout and governance plan for Gen Re.

Design the access model

You can build a least-privilege access design: Custom roles over blanket Owner, capabilities granted explicitly, SCIM groups that actually sync, and plugins distributed as controls rather than suggestions.

Govern it for a reinsurer

You can state the audit-coverage gap precisely, compensate for it with the three monitoring planes, and route the work that needs centralized audit or zero retention to an audited surface.

Prove it with numbers

You can read the dashboard, the Analytics API, and the OTel stream — find the dormant desks and the model-spend concentration, and reach for the control that fixes it.
