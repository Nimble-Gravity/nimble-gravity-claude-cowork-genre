Axos · Cowork Program

Pre-work & Homework

Each workshop is two hours of hands-on time, so a little prep beforehand and a small task afterward is what makes it stick. Ten minutes before each session, ten after — that's all it takes.

Four workshops · before & after each
Workshop 1 · Setup & Foundations

Get set up before we start

Before the session

Confirm you have Claude Cowork access on your machine. Complete the AI maturity poll. Optional warm-up: Anthropic Academy's "Introduction to Claude Cowork."

After (homework)

Finish your co-setup files if you didn't in the lab, then run one starter task and review the result. Bring a question to office hours.

Workshop 2 · Use Cowork

Bring a real task

Before the session

Have a real, document-heavy task ready and its files in one folder — a loan file to summarize, a deposit relationship to review, an alert queue to triage. Skim Use Cases by Industry.

After (homework)

Run one more real task end to end, then write down the recurring part you'd turn into a skill. You'll build it in Workshop 3.

Workshop 3 · Build a Skill

Bring a workflow to automate

Before the session

Bring one repetitive workflow worth automating — ideally the one you named after Workshop 2. Know what good output looks like and what should never happen.

After (homework)

Build a skill for a real workflow of your own using the template you shipped, and bring it to office hours.

Workshop 4 · Plugins & Rollout

Bring your skill, and your IT partners

Before the session

Bring the skill you built in Workshop 3. Invite your IT and compliance partners — the governance half of this session is for them.

After (homework)

Finalize the one-page rollout and governance plan and share it with your sponsor. Office hours continue as champions take it forward.
