What You'll Be Able to Do

From a first task to a governed rollout

Cowork takes Claude Code's agent and makes it usable by anyone: you describe a result and it works across your files to produce it. The program takes a team from "what is this" to using Cowork well, building reusable skills, and rolling it out with the governance and adoption tracking a regulated bank needs — built entirely on Claude Cowork.

Who it's for

Knowledge workers across Axos — commercial and consumer banking, lending, and treasury management, plus the risk, compliance, and operations teams behind them. No coding needed, and no prior agentic-AI experience assumed.

What you leave with

A personalized Cowork setup, working fluency on real industry tasks, one reusable skill built the way Anthropic recommends, and a governed rollout plan with an adoption dashboard design.

How it runs

A simple, repeatable rhythm

Every workshop follows the same four stages, so you always know where you are and what's next.

01
Pre-work
Ten minutes before each session — access confirmed and a real task in hand.
02
Workshop content
Two hours, hands-on: teach, a live banking demo, discussion, then a 40-minute lab that ends in a real deliverable.
03
Knowledge check
A short quiz on each workshop — pass all four to unlock your certificate.
04
Homework
A small task after each session, with office hours alongside to keep it moving.
