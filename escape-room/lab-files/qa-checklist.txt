CHECKLIST ITEMS

3. Document lists Key Actions Completed as a bulleted section.
4. Document lists Upcoming Milestones with at least one item and a target date.

7. The Recommendation in the Candidate Evaluation is exactly one of: Advance, Hold, or Decline.
8. Document ends with the line: Prepared with the Cornerstone method.
